EPIC Youth Club

Enigmatic Pursuers In Chitkara (EPIC Youth Club) was founded By Harsh Tyagi and Chander Vivek Duggal in 2015, under the jurisdiction of Department of Student Welfare(DSW) now Office of Student Affairs(OSA), Enigmatic Persuers In Chitkara. Club provide the platform you need. With a vision of giving more exposure to students also giving them a reason to step out of their hostel rooms and network with each other, learning something new everyday, taking responsibilities and facing new challenges, aimed to groom all students residing in hostel of Chitkara University, Punjab, the club takes care of all activities organised for development and growth of the students.
