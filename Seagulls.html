SEAGULLS

Insight
Seagulls club is an endeavour of Chitkara Business School, Undergraduate department, to undertake and incorporate the practical applicability and creativity of the academics. The activities undertaken by the club are aimed towards providing a perfect platform where students of all the courses offered by CBS can showcase their talent and vibrancy. The club was established by Prof. Harkiran Kaur on April 28, 2010 with a vision statement, ‘Inculcate Intelligence, creativity and farsightedness among Management Professionals’.
Role of the club
The role of the club is to collect and share information related to student welfare activities, inter-departmental activities and Intra-departmental activities taking place in the university. The students are motivated and encouraged to explore their analytical, leadership, team building and creativity skills through these activities. Our endeavour is display and appreciation of the talent. Seagulls club undertakes the activities like Cultural Activities, Creative Writing, Slogan writing, ad mad shows, Rural Mandi, dance, Drama, Group Discussion, Advertisement making, Billboard making, Photography, Quiz and many more nurturing the talents, articulation, flexibility and adaptability of students.
