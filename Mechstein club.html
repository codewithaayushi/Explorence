Mechstein Club

Mechanical Engineering Department’s first technical club, MECHSTEIN, was formed in 2002 and it was initiated by Er. Amit Goyal, faculty of Mechanical Engineering at Chitkara Institute of Engineering Technology. It was earlier named Mecque Genie.

Purpose of the club

The purpose of MECHSTEIN club is to facilitate communication between the Engineering Society, the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering and the entire Mechanical Engineering undergraduate student body in CIET. MECHSTEIN club strives to create a social atmosphere for all mechanical engineers while representing undergraduate students’ interests within the Mechanical Engineering Department. The members of the club include all Mechanical Engineering undergraduate students who are currently studying at the CIET, under the Department of Mechanical Engineering.
