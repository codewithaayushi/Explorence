Toastmasters

Public speaking is an essential part of becoming successful and being noticed in professional life. Having stage fear is one of the most common hindrance people have and seeing this, Chitkara University had introduced the Toastmasters’ Club at the University. The aim was to instill confidence among students to take on the world in poised manner.
Thanks to the efforts put in by the club members in organizing various activities, discussions and public speaking events. Students have benefitted a lot from these events as they not only get to put their public speaking skills to test, they also get to see and learn from their fellow students. Kudos to the Toastmasters’ Club for helping students hone the art of communication.
